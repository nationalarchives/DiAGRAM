Your result has been calculated from your answers to the questions combined with the data in the underlying network. This shows you the probability of successfully preserving your records in terms of the two main digital preservation outcomes: Renderability and Intellectual Control.

For example, if your score for Intellectual Control is 21, this means that out of a 100 files you are likely to have full knowledge of the material content, provenance and conditions of use for 21 of them. If your score for Renderability is 45 this means that out of a 100 files you would be able to provide a sufficiently useful representation of 45 of the original files.
